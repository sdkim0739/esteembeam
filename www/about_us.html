About EsteemBeam, the Company

Hello! We, a team of highschool girls, have created this app for you because we truly believe that having a high self-esteem and loving yourself is necessary in being successful and leading a happy life. Our team includes 6 girls: Shagun Varma, Stephanie Kim, Julia Delgado, Rafiya Awan, Maya Ostrowski, and Monica Para. We have made/coded this application from scratch and could not have done it if we, ourselves, did not have confidence in our abliities and believe in ourselves.

Our Mission: To provide services to the user to boost their self-esteem, in the end, making them love and accept themselves. When a person has self-love, one can perform better in almost all areas of life.

Our Vision: To make EsteemBeam a market leader in providing digital self-help services to digitally savvy teens and of building a loyal following of users.

We hope that this app helps you realize what an amazing, beautiful, valuable person you are and allow you to unlock the wonderous potential that you have.
